- "Improve/tune prompt"
- "ปรับ prompt"

## Focus Areas

### DeepSeek (Thai Script)
- Natural Thai narration
- Appropriate length
- Consistent tone

### Veo 3 (English Video)

- ≤8 second segments
- Cinematic quality

```

## Phase 3: Validation → Agent-4

2. Verify scene timing
3. Check output quality
```
