# Veo 3 Prompt Template

## Structure
[SUBJECT DESCRIPTION]
[ACTION/MOVEMENT]
[SETTING/ENVIRONMENT]
[LIGHTING/MOOD]
[CAMERA ANGLE/MOVEMENT]
[QUALITY TAGS]

## Example for Health Content

### Scene: Introduction
Thai woman in her early 30s, athletic build, wearing stylish workout clothes,
standing confidently with a warm smile,
in a bright modern fitness studio with large windows,
soft morning sunlight creating a motivational atmosphere,
medium shot slowly zooming in on the subject,
cinematic 4K, professional lighting, warm color grading

### Scene: Exercise
Thai woman in her early 30s, athletic build, wearing workout clothes,
doing high-intensity interval training with energetic movements,
in a well-equipped modern gym,
dynamic lighting emphasizing movement and energy,
tracking shot following the exercise movements,
cinematic 4K, action camera style, sharp focus

### Scene: Nutrition
Hands preparing a colorful healthy meal,
chopping fresh vegetables with precise movements,
in a clean modern kitchen with natural light,
bright, appetizing lighting highlighting food colors,
close-up on hands and ingredients,
high quality 4K, food photography style

### Scene: Results/Success
Thai woman in her early 30s looking happy and confident,
checking progress in mirror with satisfied expression,
in a bright bathroom or bedroom,
soft, flattering morning light,
medium shot slowly zooming out to reveal transformation,
cinematic 4K, uplifting mood, warm tones

## Keywords by Emotion

### Motivational
- determined expression
- energetic movement
- bright lighting
- uplifting atmosphere
- confident pose

### Calm
- peaceful expression
- slow, deliberate movement
- soft lighting
- serene atmosphere
- relaxed pose

### Urgent
- focused expression
- quick, purposeful movement
- dramatic lighting
- intense atmosphere
- alert pose

### Happy
- smiling expression
- light movement
- warm lighting
- cheerful atmosphere
- open pose
